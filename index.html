How we create value

Social commitment and voluntary work form the backbone of our society. That is why organisations that focus on the well-being of others are essential for our society.

Nevertheless, social associations are often confronted with considerable economic and organisational hurdles. This is the point where we, the student consultancy Saidia Consulting e.V., come in: We support social organisations with business know-how concerning organisational and economic issues.
